About The Binding of Isaac (TBOI)

When Isaac's mother started to hear God's voice asking her for a sacrifice to prove her faith, Isaac escaped and hid in the basement. But many ennemies were waiting for him down there. The Binding of Isaac is a role, shooting and action game. Help Isaac to defeat terrible monsters using wonderful items.

About TBOI Challenge Generator

This site is a TBOI Challenge Generator. The goal is to give you randomly generated challenges doable on Isaac, depending on how far you are in your game. The only thing you have to do is enter which characters and bosses you have unlocked and click on te Generate Challenge button. Then our site will give you an original challenge you can try to achieve!

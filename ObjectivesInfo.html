Objectives Information

On this page, you will be able to find basic informations about all the final bosses. The bosses are sorted by extension : Rebirth, Afterbirth, Afterbirth+ and Repentance.

Be careful, this section might contain spoilers! For further information about the bosses, you can go to this page :

Rebirth Bosses

The Binding of Isaac Rebirth is the original version of the game, it is a remake of the game The Binding of Isaac. It contains a total of 2 final bosses.

Mom's Foot and Mom's Heart

Mom's Foot is the ifrst final boss of the game. After beaten her once, you will be able to access two new floors : womb I and womb II. At womb II you'll have to fight your second final boss : Mom's Heart. When beaten 11 times, you'll be able to access either The Cathedral or Sheol.

??? (Blue Baby)

Blue Baby is the final boss of The Chest, which is accessible after beating The Cathedral and Isaac. Unlocking him requires to have beaten Isaac once.

The Lamb

The Lamb is the final boss of the Dark Room, the zone after Sheol. Unlocking The Lamb works the same as for Blue Baby, but instead of going to The Cathedral, one should go to Sheol and defeat Satan.

Afterbirth Bosses

With Afterbirth comes one new mode : the Greed Mode with one new boss Ultra Greed. One other final boss appear : Hush.

Hush

Hush is the Final boss of the ???, or Blue Womb, boss, it is unlocked by beating Mom's Heart 10 times and can be accessed by beating Mom's Heart or It Lives in less than 30 minutes.

Afterbirth + Bosses

With this extension comes the hard Greed Mode : Greedier Mode with its final boss Ultra Greedier, and one new final boss : Delirium

Ultra Greedier

Ultra Greedier is the final boss of the Greedier mode. This mode is unlocked after outting 500 coins in the Greed Donation Machine, available at the end of a Greed Mode run.

Delirium

Delirium is the main boss of The Void, which is unlocked by beating Hush once. Portals to the Void can appear after each major boss, but it has really low chances. However, defeating Hush will guarantee that it appears. The floor will have multiple boss rooms and only one will contain Delirium. Delirium's main form is stationary but teleports erratically around the room and can send projectiles but also transforms into bosses previously encountered during the run.

Repentance Bosses

With this final extension come two new final bosses : Mother and The Beast.

Mother

Mother is the final boss of The Corpse, a zone unlockable after beating Hush 3 times. One will need to go find the two pieces of the knife in Downpour and Mines, then use the knife on the flesh door after Mom's fight in the Mausoleum.

The Beast

The Beast is the final boss of Home. Home is unlocked as soon as Mother is beaten once. After beating Mom, teleport outside of the room and place the Polaroid/the Negative into the door in the first room of the floor. Then go to the starting room of each floor, ascending and coming back to the Basement then to Home. There, the fight with The Beast will start.

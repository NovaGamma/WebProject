Characters Information

On this page, you will be able to find basic informations about all the characters. The characters are sorted by extension : Rebirth, Afterbirth, Afterbirthj and Repentance.

Be careful, this section might contain spoilers! For further information about the characters, you can go to this page : Character wiki

Rebirth characters

The Binding of Isaac Rebirth is the original version of the game, it is a remake of the game The Binding of Isaac. It contains a total of 11 characters.

Isaac

Isaac is the main character of the game. He is the only character unlocked by default. He starts with three Red Heart containers and one bomb. He will also start with The D6 once it is unlocked. This dice will allow him to reroll items once every six rooms.

Isaac starts with basic stats : 3,5 damage, 1 shot speed, 1 speed, 6,5 shot speed and 0 luck.

Magdalene

Magdalene is the first unlockable character. She is unlocked by having at least seven heart containers in a run.

Magdalene starts with four red heart containers and the Yum Heart, which heals one red heart every 4 rooms. But Magdalene is rather slow. She has a basic speed of 0.85.

Cain

Cain is a character unlocked by having at least 55 coins in a run. He starts with two red hearts, one key and Lucky Foot, a passive item which gives him 1 luck and increase drop chances.

He starts with more speed and damage than most characters but has lower range and only shoot with one eye.

Judas

Judas is the character unlocked after beating Satan for the first time.

He starts with three coins and only one red heart but has the Book of Belial, which allows him to multiply his damages every three rooms.

??? (Blue Baby)

???, or Blue Baby is a character unlocked by beating Mom's Heart, the first final boss, ten times. He starts with three Soul Hearts, which are blue, and cannot acquire red hearts.

He starts with The Poop, an object which can spawn a poop once per room.

Eve

Eve can be unlocked by doing two simultaneous floors without picking up any heart. Along these items, she starts with two red hearts and is faster than most characters.

She starts with two passive items : Whore of Babylon and Dead Bird. The first one gives her big boosts when she has less than one red heart and the second spawns an attack familiar for the room each time she gets hit. She can also unlock the Razor Blade, exchanging her heart for even more damages.

Samson

Samson is unlocked by defeating two floors in a row without taking any damage. He starts with 3 Red Heart containers and Bloody Lust, which will increase his damage for the floor each time he gets hit.

Azazel

Azazel is a character unlocked by making three deals with the Devil in one run. Azazel starts with a short-ranged version of Brimstone, a lazer dealing constant damages but requiring charging.

Azazel has the ability to fly and starts with the card The Fool, that allows the player to go back to the start of the floor and three Black Hearts.

Lazarus

Lazarus is unlocked once the player has four or more soul hearts (or black hearts) at once during a run. He starts with three red heart and a double-blue pill of a random effect. He has slightly lower Range and Luck than Isaac but also has 1 extra life at the beginning of the game. His revived form has increased base statistics.

Eden

Eden is unlocked by completing Womb and beating Mom's Heart for the first time. Each time a game is started as Eden, he gets random base stats, and two randomly chosen starting items.

The Lost

The Lost is a secret character. In Rebirth, he is unlocked by completing It's complicated. In DLCs, he is unlocked by dying in a sacrificial room while holding The Missing Poster trinket.

The Lost starts with no health and cannot gain health by any means. Therefore, it will die from any damage taken. Once unlocked, he will start with Holy Mantle, allowing him to take one damage per room. The Lost can fly and has spectral tears.

Afterbirth characters

This first extension added two new playable characters.

Lilith

Lilith is unlocked by completing Greed mode with Azazel.

Lilith starts with one red heart container and two Black Hearts. Lilith starts with three items : Box of Friends Box of Friends, used to multiply familiars, the familiar Incubus and Cambion Conception to gain familiars when taking damaging. Lilith is permanently blindfolded, she cannot fire tears; she instead deals damage using her familiars.

Keeper

Keeper is unlocked by donating 1000 coins to the Greed machine, only available in Greed Mode. Keeper starts with 1 bomb and a triple shot.

Keeper uses coins as health. He starts with two Coin Hearts, loses one every time he is damaged, and heals for one coin every time he obtains a coin. All types of hearts are transformed into blue flies.

Afterbirth+ characters

The second extension added two unlockable characters.

Apollyon

He is unlocked by defeating Mega Satan for the first time. Apollyon starts with two Red Heart containers and Void, which can transform passive items into random statistics and active items that can get used ever time Void is used, once every 6 rooms.

The Forgotten

The Forgotten is a secret character. He cannot fire regular tears, and instead has a bone club that can be swung as a melee weapon or charged to be thrown. One can switch control to The Soul, a blue ghost chained to The Forgotten who fires spectral tears and flies. While controlling it, The Forgotten cannot take damage. If The Soul dies, the run will end. Any regular heart containers acquired will be turned into bone hearts, and any soul or black hearts will be given to The Soul.

The Forgotten is unlocked by It's compliacted method

Repentance characters

This last extension added two new characters as well as an alternative version for each of the 17 characters, called tainted versions.

Bethany

She is unlocked by beating Mom's Heart/It Lives in hard mode with Lazarus Lazarus without losing a life. Bethany starts with three Red Heart containers, four soul charges, and Book of Virtues, which can spawn blue flames that can shoot. Bethany is unable to utilize Soul Hearts and Black Hearts as health. When picked up, they are used to charge her book.

Jacob and Esau

They are unlocked by defeating Mother with any character. Jacob & Esau are controlled as one, move at the same speed, and use the same coins, bombs, and keys. Otherwise, they are completely independent in terms of stats, items, and health. If either Jacob or Esau die, they both die regardless of the other character's remaining health.

Tainted Characters

The tainted characters are unlocked by going to Home and using a Red Key as their original version.

Tainted Isaac

Tainted Isaac can only hold 8 passive items at a time. Tainted Isaac's current 8 passive items are visible in the top-left corner of the screen. Upon picking up a 9th passive item, the item currently selected will be dropped on a pedestal in front of Tainted Isaac.

Tainted Magdalene

Tainted Magdalene starts with 2 heart containers, 2 empty heart containers, and Yum Heart in the consumable slot. When Tainted Magdalene's health is above 2 red hearts, she will leave red creep on the floor and will lose health at the rate of half a heart per 10 seconds.

Tainted Cain

Tainted Cain starts with 2 heart containers, 1 key, and the Bag of Crafting Bag of Crafting in the consumable slot. Tainted Cain can't get items through any means other than the Bag of Crafting, attempting to do so will transform the item into an assortment of consumables. Using consumables (hearts, coins, bombs, keys...), Cain can craft items.

Tainted Judas

Tainted Judas starts with 2 black hearts and 3 coins. He starts with Dark Arts in his consumable slot. When used, Dark Arts gives +1.0 speed and makes him invulnerable to damage for a short period of time. Any enemies touched while Dark Arts is active will be frozen until the effect ends, at which point they will take damage. Tainted Judas is unable to gain Red Hearts. Health Ups will give him Black Hearts instead. Should he pick up a Bone Heart, he will be unable to fill it with red health.

Tainted ??? (Blue Baby)

He starts with 3 soul hearts, 3 poops, and Hold in the consumable slot. While playing as Tainted ???, bomb pickups are replaced by poop pickups, and his bomb counter is replaced by a poop counter which displays the number and effects of his poops. Tainted ??? can carry a maximum of 9 poops of different effects. He can hold a Poop for later usage. Poop pickups also have a chance to drop from fallen enemies, and by Tainted ??? by dealing damage.

Tainted Eve

Tainted Eve starts with 2 red hearts and 1 key. Tainted Eve starts with Sumptorium in her consumable slot. After holding down the fire button for 2 seconds, a shooting blood clot familiar that shoots tears will spawn. Each time a blood clot is spawned, half a heart is drained. Using Sumptorium will destroy all blood clots, returning the health used to create them, and damaging enemies they pass through as they travel.

Tainted Samson

Whenever Tainted Samson deals or takes damage, he will grow progressively more red. After reaching a certain point, he activates Berserk! and gains +0.40 speed, +0.75 tears, +3 damage, and will be restricted to using a jawbone melee weapon instead of his tears.

Tainted Azazel

Tainted Azazel starts with 3 black hearts and has his short Brimstone replaced with a room-wide long version that is very thin and deals less damage. If the charge is released before full, it will cause a short-range high-knockback attack that will paint the enemy faintly red and deal x1.5 damage. Furthermore, enemies affected will take full damage from his Brimstone beam. Unlike Azazel, he cannot fly.

Tainted Lazarus

Tainted Lazarus has two different states: Tainted Lazarus and Dead Tainted Lazarus. Tainted Lazarus starts with 3 red hearts, whereas Dead Tainted Lazarus starts with 2 soul hearts. Both characters have their own attributes, as well as their own items and trinkets. Whenever a room is cleared, Tainted Lazarus swaps between the two forms. Tainted Lazarus starts with Flip in his consumable slot, which can be used to swap state immediately once every 6 rooms.

Tainted Eden

Like Eden, Tainted Eden starts with random stats, health, possibly some pickups, an active item and a passive item. Every time they take damage, all of their stats, items (passive and active), their trinket and their currently-held card or pill get re-rolled.

Tainted Lost

Tainted Lost is just like The Lost, he has no health and flies, but instead of Holy Mantle, it starts with a Holy Card. It also starts with a 1.3x damage multiplier, giving it higher starting damage and a higher efficacy for damage-increasing items. He cannot find Health Up items. Cards and Runes have a 10% chance of being turned into a Holy Card.

Tainted Lilith

She starts with one red heart container, 2 black hearts. Like Normal Lilith, she can't fire tears. Instead of tears, Tainted Lilith launches a demonic baby attached via umbilical cord out of her stomach that can be recalled after attacking, dealing 3x damage to enemies it hits. The Baby also will fire tears which deal 0.75x damage. It returns to Tainted Lilith once fire is released.

Tainted Keeper

Tainted Keeper starts with 2 coin hearts and 1 bomb. Tainted Keeper shares the same mechanics with the regular Keeper. Tainted Keeper shoots four tears at a time. Whenever an enemy is defeated, a random coin is dropped that will vanish quickly. Most items encountered when playing as Tainted Keeper must first be paid for with coins, including Deals with the Devil, Angel Rooms, Boss Room items, and Treasure Room items.

Tainted Apollyon

Tainted Apollyon starts with 2 heart containers, Abyss in the consumable slot and 1 red Abyss locust familiar. When the Abyss is used, items in current room are destroyed and turned into further locust familiars. Locusts familiars charge in the direction Tainted Apollyon is firing until they hit something, dealing Tainted Apollyon's damage per tick.

Tainted The Forgotten

The character is split into two parts. Tainted Soul resembles The Soul and is directly controlled, but cannot attack in any form. Tainted Soul cannot obtain red heart containers. Tainted Forgotten is completely invulnerable, immobile and does not have a health bar. Colliding with Tainted Forgotten will cause Tainted Soul to hold it over its head, and pressing any of the fire buttons will throw the skeletal body in a given direction to attack.

Tainted Bethany

Tainted Bethany starts with 3 soul hearts, 6 blood charges, and Lemegeton in the consumable slot. Tainted Bethany cannot obtain red heart containers. When picked up, red hearts instead can be consumed to use Tainted Bethany's activated item.

Tainted Jacob

Tainted Jacob spawns with 3 red heart containers and Anima Sola in his consumable slot. Anima Sola freezes the nearest enemy for 5 seconds. However, he is pursued through the entire game by Dark Esau, who will try to charge into and kill Tainted Jacob. Anima Sola always affects Dark Esau if he is on screen. If Dark Esau touches Tainted Jacob or if Dark Esau dies, Tainted Jacob "dies" and becomes The Lost.
